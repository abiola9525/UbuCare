Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to your most common questions regarding our TeleHealth platform

What is TeleHealth and how does it work?

TeleHealth allows patients to consult with healthcare professionals remotely via video calls, phone calls, or chat. The platform provides access to doctors, pharmacists, and therapists, ensuring healthcare is accessible from the comfort of your home.

How do I schedule a consultation?

To schedule a consultation, simply log in to your account, choose the doctor or service you need, and select a time slot that works for you. You can choose video, phone, or text-based consultations, depending on the doctor's availability.

Is my health data secure on the platform?

Yes, your health data is stored securely using end-to-end encryption, ensuring your information is protected during consultations. We follow all legal and regulatory standards to maintain the confidentiality and safety of your data.

Can I access a prescription through the platform?

Yes, after a consultation, if the doctor prescribes medication, you will receive an electronic prescription which you can take to a pharmacy for fulfillment. Some services also allow for prescription delivery.

Are the consultations covered by insurance?

This depends on your insurance plan. We recommend checking with your insurance provider to see if TeleHealth services are covered. We are working on partnerships with multiple insurance providers to facilitate this.

What happens if I miss my scheduled consultation?

If you miss your consultation, the session may be rescheduled. However, please note that a missed appointment might incur a cancellation fee, depending on the platform’s terms and conditions.
